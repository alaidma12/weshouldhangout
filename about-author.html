The Instigator

Amber is the person who sends the calendar invite, picks the restaurant, and follows up when you go quiet for too long. As an experienced learning leader, she designs courses that don't just inform — they move people. Behavioral science, sharp storytelling, and a healthy refusal to accept "we should hang out soon" as a complete sentence.

She's led learning and development teams across industries, building programs that are equal parts engaging and actionable. This course exists because she noticed a pattern — in research, in her own life, and probably in yours too.

When she's not designing learning, Amber's exploring another country (15 and counting), running half-marathons, or learning Spanish and Japanese because two languages felt more interesting than one.
